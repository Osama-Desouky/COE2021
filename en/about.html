Every step we take is driven by our passion for excellence

“Tamayouz Company” is the leading company in the field of technical and vocational training services in the Kingdom. It is the link between the best providers of training and education services locally and globally, funding bodies, legislators, and labor market regulators, in addition to the supervision and evaluation bodies of education and training on the one hand, and between the owners of Business in the public and private sectors On the other hand, our first journey towards excellence started in 2013, we started it by creating a completely new business model for the work environment in the Kingdom, then we proceeded towards creativity in response to the needs of the technical and vocational training sector, leading to innovating solutions and training services, which Meet the employers' need of qualified national cadres.

The company was established by the General Corporation for Technical and Vocational Training and the Takamul Business Services Holding Company, to take it upon itself to provide its clients with all innovative technical and training solutions, in addition to providing all advisory and training services that are in line with business requirements, we operate and develop training facilities, design training programs, we By training and supervising the training process, to lead future generations towards the future, fulfill the needs and requirements of all sectors of the Saudi labor market, and enable national cadres to compete to become an effective element in achieving the Kingdom’s ambitious 2030 vision with its three pillars: a vibrant society, a prosperous economy, and an ambitious nation.
